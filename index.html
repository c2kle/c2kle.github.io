About Us

The Triangle Tennis Academy was established in 2008 and currently owned by coach Kabiru Ibrahim, he is a professional tennis coach and former ATP/ITF pro. Coach Kabiru’s passion and vision for tennis has led him to offering a full-service tennis academy. Camps, customized training, advanced junior programs, tournament groups, ladder matches, summer camps, private, group, adult and team lessons. New students should contact for an evaluation and assessment. Clinics or Matchplay Clinics, Tuesdays and Thursdays at 4:30pm to 6:00pm in Durham, NC. Matchplay Clinics, Saturdays and Sundays in Raleigh and Durham. Please contact Triangle Tennis Academy with any questions at (919)-413-9531.

Services

Private Lessons

Private lessons are personalized tennis sessions with Coach Kabiru. The sessions are generally for individual personal analysis and mostly works on player's personal progression and development. Lessons are generally broken down into several sessions for each player, and based on expectations or goals, long or short term, Coach K will customize lessons to meet each players needs.

Clinics

Clinic are generally for playersor groups of players, betweeen 4 to 8 players looking for matchplay or pointplay with students or players. The clinic environment facilitates variety and opportunity for each individual to practice against other players with different styles of play. Clinics are generally for those looking for a group experience and also the opportunity to learn or compete with others.

Sign Up

Full-Time

Full time students at Triangle Tennis Academy committed to a full time program enjoy a 5-6 day/week of tennis program that provides lessons, match play, and a yearly guide to long term goals. The full time players have unlimited access to training and generally have more court time with Coach K. Full time players have a weekly 5 day commitment to both lessons and matchplay hours with tournament supervision by request.
